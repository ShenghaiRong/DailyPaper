### 1. World Action Verifier: Self-Improving World Models via Forward-Inverse Asymmetry
- **作者**: Yuejiang Liu†,∗
- **机构**: 未知
- **链接**: [arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01985v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01985v1/pdf)
- **来源**: 📄 arXiv 关键词检索

![](https://arxiv.org/html/2604.01985v1/x1.png)

- **核心方法**: 将action-conditioned state prediction分解为state plausibility和action reachability两部分，分别验证。WAV利用两个asymmetry：（1）action-free data更丰富，用diverse subgoal generator；（2）action-relevant features维度更低，用sparse inverse model推断动作。通过cycle consistency（generated subgoals → inferred actions → forward rollouts）来验证world model的预测错误，引导探索到高价值区域。
- **对比方法/Baselines**: OOS（out-of-state baseline）、IDM（inverse dynamics model）、TLCM、SAILOR、MiniGrid、RoboMimic、ManiSkill等。WAV在9个任务上实现2x sample efficiency，downstream policy提升18%。
- **借鉴意义**: 对做world model的人有用——如果训练数据主要是action-labeled的，world model对suboptimal action的预测会很弱。WAV提供了一个self-improving框架，可以在部署后持续收集无标签数据提升robustness。
- **锐评**: 思路非常漂亮。forward-inverse asymmetry是true insight——验证state plausibility不需要action，但验证action reachability只需要action-relevant的subset，这两个问题都比完整预测简单。理论分析也扎实，sample efficiency提升2x很合理。但实验规模有点小，MiniGrid、RoboMimic、ManiSkill都是仿真环境，真实世界的generalization还没验证。另外，inverse model的质量很关键，如果inference不准，整个verification pipeline会fail。
- **关联笔记**: [[World Model]]、[[World Action Verifier]]

### 2. Safety, Security, and Cognitive Risks in World Models
- **作者**: Parmar, Manoj
- **机构**: 未知
- **链接**: [arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01346v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01346v1/pdf)
- **来源**: 📄 arXiv 关键词检索

![](https://arxiv.org/html/2604.01346v1/x1.png)

- **核心方法**: 这是一篇survey，系统梳理world model的安全、安全和认知风险。引入两个formal definitions：trajectory persistence（攻击在rollout中的持续性）和representational risk（latent space的脆弱性）。提出5-tier attacker capability taxonomy，扩展MITRE ATLAS和OWASP LLM Top 10到world model stack。提供empirical proof-of-concept：trajectory-persistent adversarial attacks，在GRU-RSSM上实现2.26x amplification，adversarial fine-tuning后-59.5%；stochastic RSSM proxy 0.65x；DreamerV3 checkpoint确认action drift。
- **对比方法/Baselines**: Zeng et al. (world model safety survey)——仅关注robustness和distribution shift，本文加了formal attacker capability taxonomy、trajectory persistence和representational risk的formal definition、empirical proof-of-concept experiment、alignment和cognitive risk categories（未涵盖）。
- **借鉴意义**: 这篇很重要——world model正被部署到safety-critical systems（robotics、autonomous vehicles、agentic AI），但security研究几乎空白。文章给出了一个unified threat model，可以用来做threat modeling和风险评估。
- **锐评**: 终于有人认真对待world model的safety了。trajectory persistence的formal definition很有用——攻击会持续放大rollout error，这点在长horizon planning中是致命的。Attacker capability taxonomy（5-tier）覆盖了从data poisoning到runtime manipulation，比简单分类更实用。Empirical proof-of-concept证明了trajectory-persistent attack是real threat：GRU-RSSM 2.26x amplification不是小问题。但文章主要是high-level threat modeling，concrete mitigations（adversarial hardening、alignment engineering）都是hand-wavy，没给可操作的implementation guidance。另外，四个deployment scenarios的illustration有点abstract，没给具体defense algorithm。

### 3. LeWorldModel: Stable End-to-End Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture from Pixels
- **作者**: Maes, Lucas, Lidec, Quentin Le, Scieur, Damien, LeCun, Yann, Balestriero, Randall
- **机构**: 1Mila & Université de Montréal  2New York University  3Samsung SAIL   4Brown University
- **链接**: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19312) | [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.19312)
- **来源**: 🔥 HF Trending，⬆️ 20

![](https://arxiv.org/html/2603.19312v2/x1.png)

- **核心方法**: LeWorldModel (LeWM)是第一个end-to-end JEPA，直接从raw pixels训练，只用两个loss terms：next-embedding prediction loss + Gaussian-distributed latent embeddings regularizer。将tunable hyperparameters从6个降到1个（相比existing end-to-end alternative）。~15M参数，单GPU几小时训练，规划速度比foundation-model-based world models快48x。
- **对比方法/Baselines**: DINO-WM（需要pre-trained encoders）、PLDM。LeWM在2D和3D control tasks（PushT、Two-Room、OGBench-Cube等）上保持competitive，但planning speed和stability更好。Latent space通过probing物理 quantities（position、velocity等）证明encoding了meaningful structure，surprise evaluation确认能检测physically implausible events。
- **借鉴意义**: 对做JEPA的人有用——证明了end-to-end JEPA可以stable训练，不需要EMA、不需要auxiliary supervision、不需要pre-trained encoders。15M参数小规模，training成本低，planning快48x（相比DINO-WM）。
- **锐评**: 这篇质量很高。End-to-end JEPA + two-loss formulation是clean solution，把DINO-WM的复杂multi-term loss简化了，训练更稳定。~15M参数、几小时训练、48x planning speed，这个efficiency很impressive。Latent space的probing证明encoding了物理结构，surprise evaluation能检测implausible events，这些结果有力。但tasks规模偏小（MiniGrid、2D grid worlds、simple 3D manipulations），在更复杂场景（如long-horizon manipulation、navigation）的generalization还没验证。另外，虽然说不用pre-trained encoders，但初始化策略没提，如果random init收敛慢怎么办？
- **关联笔记**: [[JEPA]]、[[DreamerV4]]、[[World Model]]

### 12. DriveGATr: Efficient Equivariant Transformer for Self-Driving Agent Modeling
- **作者**: Scott Xu1,2
- **机构**: Waabi1, University of Toronto2
- **链接**: [arXiv](http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01466v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.01466v1)
- **来源**: 📄 arXiv 关键词检索

![](https://arxiv.org/html/2604.01466v1/x1.png)

- **核心方法**: DriveGATr是transformer-based agent modeling architecture，实现SE(2)-equivariance但无explicit pairwise relative positional encodings的计算开销。Inspired by geometric deep learning，scene elements编码为2D projective geometric algebra ($\mathbb{R}^*_{2,0,1}$) multivectors，标准attention在multivectors之间处理，eliminating need for costly pairwise RPE。

![](https://arxiv.org/html/2604.02330v1/x1.png)

- **核心方法**: ActionParty是action-controllable multi-subject world model for generative video games。Introduces subject state tokens (latent variables persistently capture state of each subject)，jointly modeling state tokens和video latents with spatial biasing mechanism，disentangle global video frame rendering from individual action-controlled subject updates。Melting Pot benchmark上evaluation，controlling up to seven players simultaneously across 46 diverse environments。
- **对比方法/Baselines**: ActionParty demonstrates first video world model capable of controlling up to seven players simultaneously，significant improvements in action-following accuracy和identity consistency，enabling robust autoregressive tracking。

![](https://arxiv.org/html/2604.02226v1/x1.png)

- **核心方法**: Adaptive Safety through Knowledge (ASK) combines smaller LMs with trained RL policies enhance OOD generalization without retraining。Employ Monte Carlo Dropout assess uncertainty，query LM for action suggestions only when uncertainty exceeds threshold。Selective use preserves efficiency of existing policies while leveraging LM reasoning in uncertain situations。

- **借鉴意义**: 对做RL的人有用——LMs有world knowledge但high cost、limited autonomous planning，neuro-symbolic integration需要careful orchestration。
- **锐评**: Idea有价值。Monte Carlo Dropout评估uncertainty，threshold gating LM calls——selective use preserve efficiency是reasonable design。FrozenLake transfer task reward 0.95不错。但in-domain no improvement有点concerning——如果uncertainty estimator不准，threshold gating会hurt performance。另外，FrozenLake is simple gridworld，generalization to complex tasks（如Atari、robotics）如何？
